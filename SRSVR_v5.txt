**Specific Recommendations for Improvement:**

*   **Clarify Notification Delivery (NOT-1.0):** Specify mechanism for handling a failed push notification.  Will it retry?  Will it log the failure?
*   **Maintenance Diagnostics:** Define source, timing, trigger, and type of maintenance alerts and diagnostics.
*   **Logical Database Requirements:** Expand Section 5.3.4 in SRS to specify the required database entities, relationships, constraints, data retention and compliance.
*   **Address Annex H:** Include a separate section or appendix that explicitly addresses the life cycle data objectives outlined in Annex H of IEEE/EIA 12207.0-1996.
*   **Consider Security Audit Strategy:** As CM-1.3 mentioned the acceptance of the credit card data and the conduct of PCI DSS security scans, document more of the app's security strategy.
